As seen from the output confidence intervals, it is first noticed that chas^2 and chas^3 do not have coefficients. That is due to that chas is a binary variable, whose squre or cube is essentially itself, so in this case chas, chas^2 and chas^3 are linearly related, and therefore the latter two polynomial terms are not fitted in the model. chas put aside, several polynomial terms exhibit correlation with crim, whose 95% coefficient confidence intervals exclude zero. For example, nox^2 and nox^3.
